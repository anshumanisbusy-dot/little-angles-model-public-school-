About Us

Where Dreams Begin

Welcome to Little Angels Model Public School — a beacon of quality education in Chakla Nirmali, Supaul, Bihar. For over 20 glorious years, we have been shaping young minds into confident, capable, and compassionate human beings.

Our English Medium school is equipped with modern infrastructure, experienced teachers, and a nurturing environment where every child is encouraged to discover their unique potential and soar to new heights.

With 1000+ students and 50+ dedicated staff members, we are a family that believes every child is special and deserves the very best start in life.
